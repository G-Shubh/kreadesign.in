DESIGN BRIEF & CONTRACT

To start creating the project, we sign a contract and show you examples of finished projects. Usually we hold several meetings and find out your ideas and discuss all the requirements to the future design

DESIGN LAYOUT

We offer several layouts and agree on the best one in which we fix all your wishes for floor planning, furniture layout.

VISUALISATION

We present you photorealistic images of the finished work which includes all the details of exterior and interior with all the furniture. The creating process includes making the virtual 3D-MODEL of the design in the real size, which helps to see the final result more clear. This format is comfortable both for us and for you.

PROjECT CRITIQUE

After creating the 3-D MODEL of the area, all the corrections and improving we present all the result we got to the client. During the presentation, the recommendations about the project's realizations are given by the designers.

MATERIALS SELECTION

Working on the project, at the same time we create the specification of the equiment, decorative materials and furniture used in the project. If the creation of the author's constructions or furniture elements take place, the client gets a complete set of the technical documents.

DOCUMENTATION

We present a complete documentation package for implementation of the project.

IMPLEMENTATION

The work on the project does not end when the drawing package and visualization are admitted by the customer. During the repair and construction works we control all the key processess: from the quality of operatios and terms of furniture and equipment delivery to timely occupancy.

YOUR NEW HOME

We bring the final decorative touches. You accept the result.
